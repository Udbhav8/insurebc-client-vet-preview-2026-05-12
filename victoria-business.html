Commercial coverage taxonomy

Eight coverages cover almost every BC small-business risk. We build a custom stack from these, priced across the broker market.

Commercial General Liability (CGL) - $2M-$5M liability for bodily injury, property damage and personal injury caused by your operations, products or premises.
Commercial Property - Building, contents, stock, tenant improvements and outdoor signs. Replacement cost preferred over ACV.
Business Interruption - Lost gross earnings and ongoing fixed expenses while you rebuild after a covered loss. Indemnity periods of 12, 18 or 24 months.
Professional Liability (E&O) - Claims your professional advice, design or services caused a client financial loss. Mandatory for many regulated professions.
Cyber Liability - Privacy breach response, ransomware, business email compromise, regulatory penalties, social engineering, cyber business interruption.
Commercial Auto / Fleet - ICBC commercial Autoplan plus excess liability ($2M-$5M) on company vehicles, contractor trucks and fleet.
Equipment Breakdown - Boilers, HVAC, refrigeration, kitchen equipment and specialty machinery, including spoilage of refrigerated stock.
Employment Practices (EPL) - Wrongful dismissal, harassment, discrimination and other employment-related claims by employees or applicants.

FAQ

How much does business insurance cost in Victoria, BC?
Most Victoria small businesses pay $500-$1,800 per year for general liability plus commercial property combined. Trades and food service typically run $1,200-$3,500. Solo tech consultants and online sellers can start around $400. The number moves with revenue, payroll, premises value, claims history, and the specific coverages you carry (CGL only vs CGL plus property vs the full stack including BI, professional liability, cyber and commercial auto).
What kind of insurance does my business need in BC?
Every BC business should carry Commercial General Liability (CGL) at a minimum, typically $2M or $5M. Add Commercial Property if you own or rent premises with equipment or stock, Business Interruption to cover lost revenue after a loss, Professional Liability (E&O) if you give advice or design work, Cyber Liability if you hold customer data, Commercial Auto if you drive for work, and Equipment Breakdown if you depend on HVAC, refrigeration or specialty machinery. We tailor the stack to the business.
Is commercial general liability mandatory in BC?
CGL is not provincially mandated for all businesses, but it is effectively required: most commercial landlords, municipal permit processes, prime contractors, conference venues and major customers require proof of $2M-$5M CGL before they will do business with you. Operating without it is also a personal-asset exposure if a customer or visitor is injured.
Do I need professional liability insurance as a consultant?
Yes if your work involves giving advice, design or specifications that a client relies on. Professional liability (errors and omissions, or E&O) covers claims that your professional work caused a financial loss to a client. Tech consultants, marketing agencies, accountants, lawyers, real estate brokers, mortgage brokers, and many health practitioners need it. For regulated professions (lawyers, accountants, real estate brokers, mortgage brokers) E&O is a licensing requirement.
Does my home insurance cover my home-based business?
Almost never, beyond a token sub-limit (often $2,500-$5,000 for business property on premises and zero for off-premises). Standard home policies exclude commercial activity. If you run a home-based business in Victoria, you need either a Home-Based Business endorsement on the home policy or a standalone commercial policy. We quote both.
How much does $2 million liability cost for a contractor in BC?
A $2M CGL policy for a Greater Victoria sole-proprietor contractor with $100K-$300K in annual revenue typically runs $600-$1,200 per year. Larger general contractors with employees, subcontractors and larger revenue can be $2,000-$6,000+. WorkSafeBC is separate and mandatory for anyone with employees in BC. We quote contractors with Intact, Northbridge, Travelers and specialty markets.
